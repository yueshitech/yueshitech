Compliance & Safety

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2024

1. Global Privacy & Data Adaptation

Beijing Yueshi Internet Technology Co., Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. Our data practices are designed to comply with global standards:

  • GDPR (Europe): We process personal data of EEA residents under the strict requirements of the General Data Protection Regulation.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): We respect the privacy rights of California residents, providing transparent disclosures about data collection and sales (we do not sell your personal data).
  • LGPD (Brazil) & PIPL (China): We adapt our systems to meet regional data localization and protection requirements.

2. Global Age Management & Children's Policy

We strictly adhere to the Google Play Families Policy and the Apple App Store Safety Guidelines regarding children's privacy.

  • COPPA Compliance: In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • Age Gating: For apps targeting a general audience, we implement neutral age-gating mechanisms to ensure age-appropriate data collection and ad targeting.
  • Children-Directed Apps: For apps specifically designed for children, we do not use behavioral advertising and only collect technical identifiers necessary for the core app experience.

3. Store & Monetization Platform Adaptation

Our data collection is adapted to the technical and policy requirements of our distribution and monetization partners:

  • App Stores: We comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and Google Play's Data Safety section requirements.
  • Ad Platforms: Our monetization partners (such as AdMob, AppLovin, Unity Ads, and ironSource) may collect device-level identifiers (IDFA, GAID) solely for advertising measurement and fraud prevention, subject to your opt-out rights.

4. Contact & Data Subject Rights

If you wish to exercise your right to access, delete, or port your personal data, please contact our privacy office at support@yueshitech.com. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
